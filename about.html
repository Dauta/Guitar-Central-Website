About Us

Guitar Central is a multi-purpose music center. Mostly specializing on guitars and guitar related equipment. It was founded by Irakli Dautashvili in spring of 2016. Providing the best equipment and services, Guitar Central always has prioritized quality over everything else.

We are located in Blagoevgrad, Bulgaria. However, the high demand has inspired us to open branches in new locations. We are happy to announce that the new Guitar Centrals are coming soon to Western Europe and North America.
